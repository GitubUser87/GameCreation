CONTROLS:
Keyboard:
W + Up Arrow - Forward
A + Left Arrow - Left
D + Right Arrow - Right
S + Down Arrow - Down
Mouse - Camera Movement
E - Interact
Space Bar - Jump
P - Pause

Controller:
Left Control Stick - WASD - Movement
Right Control Stick - Camera Movement
A - Jump
X - Interact
Start Button - Pause

NOTES:
All buttons on widgets can only be used by mouse and cursor. (This relates to the Keypad and Pause menu widgets.)
Some of the dialogue boxs are a bit broken due to scaling issues and a lack of time.
